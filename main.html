Team IEEE UVCE is proud to present IEEE UVCE Computer Society’s inaugural annual Hackathon on the " " following the 102nd year of our esteemed institution. Terms And Conditions


  • The registration fees, once paid, cannot be refunded under any circumstances.
  • Any violation of the “Eligibility Criteria” will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
  • The participants will have to heed to the instructions given by the CodeFury organising committee and the CodeFury volunteering committee.
  • Participants found downloading entire templates will be disqualified. But participants are permitted to use the internet (for small blocks of code to implement a certain functionality) to aid them in building the webpage/app.
  • The final winner will be chosen based on their presentation and the innovation/idea behind the webpage/app.
  • The final decision is based on the sole discretion of the panel of judges.
